About the Company

We are a team of 20 healthcare workers, including Family Doctors, Nurse practitioners, Licensed Practical Nurse and Medical Office Assistants who are committed to provide our Richmond community and beyond with the highest quality of healthcare.

BC Health & Wellness works to bring both physicians and allied health professionals to care for our community in Richmond. We offer consultations by appointment, a full family practice, state-of-the art equipment, and a modern, comfortable environment for you and your family. From our friendly, respectful front-office staff to our highly-trained family physicians we hope that you will find our clinic welcoming and comfortable. We strive for excellence in all aspects of medical care and will provide you and your family with consistently high-quality service.

Our medical clinic offers family medicine services to:
1) Patients with a British Columbia CareCard
2) Patients covered by ICBC or WorkSafe BC
3) Tourists, visitors, international students, foreign workers and others who do not have a vaild British Columbia CareCard
